# Week 02 — Taproot Structure and Script Paths

Aligns with [LAB cohort Week 2](../../LAB.md#cohorts) (Mar 1 — Hands-on Taproot Coding).

---

## Objective

Understand how Taproot commits to multiple spending conditions and how
key-path vs script-path spending changes Bitcoin transaction structure.

---

## Questions

1. **Long-term tradeoffs of Schnorr** — What do we gain, and what new assumptions do we introduce?

2. **Key tweaking as commitment** — Why is this commitment, not encryption?

3. **Key-path vs Script-path** — What fundamentally changes in witness and privacy behavior?

4. **Merkle sibling order** — What breaks if sibling ordering changes?

5. **Script-path disclosure** — What does a script-path spend inevitably reveal?
